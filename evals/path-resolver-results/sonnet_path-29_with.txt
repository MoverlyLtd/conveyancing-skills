`/propertyPack/rightsAndInformalArrangements/sharedContributions`

This path handles shared costs and contributions with neighbors, including drainage maintenance costs, shared driveway upkeep, and other jointly-maintained services or facilities.

If you have documentation of the drainage sharing arrangement (like a formal agreement or deed), that would go in the `attachments` sub-path:
`/propertyPack/rightsAndInformalArrangements/sharedContributions/attachments`
