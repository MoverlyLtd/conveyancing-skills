The path to record the boundary ownership is:

`/propertyPack/legalBoundaries/ownership/uniformBoundaries`

This has sub-paths for each boundary side:

- `.../left` - owner of the left boundary (Seller/Shared/Neighbour/Not known)
- `.../right` - owner of the right boundary
- `.../rear` - owner of the rear boundary 
- `.../front` - owner of the front boundary

If the boundaries are not uniform, you can upload a boundary plan to the `.../attachments` path instead.
