To check for potential sale delay factors, I would look in the following PDTF paths:

/propertyPack/delayFactors/hasDelayFactors/attachments

This path contains evidence of any factors that could delay the sale, such as:
- Outstanding planning issues
- Unfinished building works
- Ongoing disputes or complaints
- Difficulties obtaining required consents or approvals
- Issues with chain position or onward purchase

The /propertyPack/completionAndMoving path also has relevant information:

/propertyPack/completionAndMoving/moveRestrictionDates
- Dates the seller is unable to move

/propertyPack/completionAndMoving/sufficientToRepayAllMortgages 
- Whether the sale proceeds will be sufficient to clear all existing mortgages

So checking those key paths would give a good overview of any potential delay factors for this sale.
