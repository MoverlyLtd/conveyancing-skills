The path to record whether the property has mains water is:

`/propertyPack/waterAndDrainage/water/mainsWater`

This path has the following sub-fields:

- `mainsWater` - a boolean field to indicate if the property has mains water supply
- `stopcock/attachments` - for uploading a photo of the stopcock
- `waterMeter/attachments` - for uploading a photo of the water meter

So the full path to record mains water availability and provide supporting documents would be:

`/propertyPack/waterAndDrainage/water/mainsWater`
`/propertyPack/waterAndDrainage/water/stopcock/attachments`
`/propertyPack/waterAndDrainage/water/waterMeter/attachments`
