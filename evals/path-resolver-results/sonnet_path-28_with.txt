For a public right of way, you have two paths:

**Main path:**
`/propertyPack/rightsAndInformalArrangements/rightsOrArrangements/publicRightOfWay/attachments`

This is where you upload the route plan showing the right of way.

**Boolean indicator:**
`/propertyPack/rightsAndInformalArrangements/rightsOrArrangements/publicRightOfWay` 

This records whether there IS a public right of way (true/false), and the `/attachments` sub-path is where you upload the actual route plan document.

So you'd set the boolean to `true` to indicate there's a right of way, then upload the route plan to the `attachments` array at that same path.
