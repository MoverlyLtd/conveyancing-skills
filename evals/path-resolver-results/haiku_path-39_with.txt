The correct PDTF path to upload a photograph of the gas meter is:

/propertyPack/heating/heatingSystem/centralHeatingDetails/centralHeatingFuel/gasMeter/attachments

This path is under the `/propertyPack/heating` section, which covers details about the property's heating system. The `centralHeatingDetails` node contains information about the central heating fuel source, and the `gasMeter` node is where you would upload a photograph of the gas meter.
