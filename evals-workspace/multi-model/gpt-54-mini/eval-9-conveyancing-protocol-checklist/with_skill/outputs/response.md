Below is a **pre-exchange checklist** for a **buyer’s conveyancer** at **Stage B (Pre-Exchange), Law Society Conveyancing Protocol 2019**. I’ve included the key protocol items and the critical practical checks you should have confirmed before exchange.

**Status legend:**  
- **Pass** = confirmed/completed  
- **Fail** = issue identified  
- **Pending** = not yet confirmed

1. **Title checks complete** — **Pending**  
   *Check the title, plan, contract, and any restrictive covenants, easements, mortgages, and special conditions are all understood and acceptable.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 4

2. **Replies to enquiries satisfactory** — **Pending**  
   *Make sure all outstanding enquiries have been answered properly and there are no unresolved issues affecting title or completion.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 3 and Stage B, para 6

3. **Search results are current and satisfactory** — **Pending**  
   *Confirm searches are recent enough (commonly within 6 months) and show no unacceptable adverse entries.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 11

4. **Mortgage offer is in place, valid, and all conditions are met** — **Pending**  
   *Check the mortgage offer has been issued, is still valid for exchange/completion, and any lender conditions have been satisfied.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 5 and Stage B, para 12

5. **Deposit funds are cleared and available** — **Pending**  
   *Confirm the client has provided cleared funds for the deposit and that you are ready to transfer it on exchange if required.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 9

6. **Buildings insurance is arranged to start from exchange/completion as required** — **Pending**  
   *Ensure insurance is in place from the correct risk date, usually exchange for the buyer.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage C, para 8

7. **Signed contract is received from the client** — **Pending**  
   *Make sure the signed contract is held and matches the agreed terms.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 8

8. **Contract price, completion date, and special conditions are agreed** — **Pending**  
   *Confirm the parties have agreed the contract terms and completion date, and any amendments are acceptable.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 7

9. **Deposit request and completion funding plan are in place** — **Pending**  
   *Check you have requested the deposit from the buyer and are satisfied the remainder will be available for completion.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 9 and Stage C, para 3

10. **Lender reporting completed and certificate of title ready** — **Pending**  
   *If lending, ensure the lender has been reported to and the certificate of title can be submitted on exchange/completion as required.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 5 and Stage C, para 5

11. **Fixture and fittings list agreed** — **Pending**  
   *Confirm the TA10 is agreed and matches what is being sold/bought.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 10

12. **Contract pack is complete and properly reviewed** — **Pending**  
   *Confirm the contract, title documents, property information forms, and any relevant replies are all in order.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage B, para 1

13. **Identity and source of funds checks completed** — **Pending**  
   *Make sure AML/KYC checks are done and source of funds evidence is satisfactory.*  
   **Protocol ref:** Stage A, para 2 and Stage A, para 3

14. **General protocol requirements confirmed** — **Pending**  
   *Verify the other side’s conveyancer, use the latest Standard Conditions of Sale, and follow the telephone exchange formula / Code for Completion by Post.*  
   **Protocol ref:** General, paras (a)–(g)

**Before you exchange, the key “must be satisfied” items are:**
- searches current and satisfactory  
- mortgage offer valid and conditions met  
- deposit cleared  
- buildings insurance arranged  
- signed contracts in hand  
- title checks complete  
- enquiries answered satisfactorily
