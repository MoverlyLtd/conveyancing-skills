## CLC Requirements for Client Complaints

As a CLC-regulated firm, you must handle this complaint under **Principle 3 (Act in the Best Interests of Clients)**, specifically **Outcome 3.6**: "Handle complaints fairly and promptly."

### Immediate CLC Compliance Steps

| # | Requirement | Action Needed |
|---|-------------|---------------|
| 1 | **Acknowledge promptly** | Send written acknowledgment within 8 days of receiving the complaint |
| 2 | **Investigate fairly** | Conduct thorough review of the transaction file and timeline |
| 3 | **Respond substantively** | Provide detailed response within 8 weeks maximum |
| 4 | **Record in complaints register** | Log complaint details, investigation, and resolution |
| 5 | **Inform of escalation rights** | Tell client they can refer to Legal Ombudsman if unsatisfied |

### CLC's Outcomes-Focused Approach

The CLC doesn't prescribe exact procedures — you must **deliver the outcome** of fair and prompt handling. Your firm should have internal procedures that achieve this.

### Key CLC Distinctions

**Important**: CLC complaints procedures differ from SRA/Law Society procedures. The CLC Code of Conduct prevails over any subsidiary guidance where there's conflict.

### Legal Ombudsman Referral Rights

Under **Principle 4 (Deal with Regulators and Ombudsmen)**, if the client refers to the Legal Ombudsman, you must:
- **Outcome 4.3**: Cooperate fully with their investigation
- Respond to their enquiries promptly
- Implement any directions they give
