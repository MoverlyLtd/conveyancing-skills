For a £510,000 flat in England or Northern Ireland, as a first-time buyer, the SDLT would be **£15,500**.

Breakdown:
- £0–£125,000 at 0% = £0
- £125,001–£250,000 at 2% = £2,500
- £250,001–£510,000 at 5% = £13,000

**Total SDLT: £15,500**
**Effective rate: 3.03%**

Important note: first-time buyer relief does **not** apply here because the purchase price is above the £500,000 limit.

SDLT is normally due within **14 days of completion**.
