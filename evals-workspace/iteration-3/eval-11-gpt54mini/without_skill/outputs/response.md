For a first-time buyer purchasing a flat for **£510,000** in **England or Northern Ireland**, the **stamp duty land tax (SDLT)** would be **£15,500**.

Why:
- First-time buyer relief only applies up to **£500,000**.
- At **£510,000**, you **do not qualify** for first-time buyer relief, so the **standard residential rates** apply.

Breakdown:
- 0% on the first **£125,000** = **£0**
- 2% on **£125,001 to £250,000** = **£2,500**
- 5% on **£250,001 to £510,000** = **£13,000**

**Total SDLT: £15,500**
