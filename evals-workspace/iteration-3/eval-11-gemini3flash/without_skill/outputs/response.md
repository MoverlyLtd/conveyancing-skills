As a first-time buyer in England or Northern Ireland purchasing a flat for £510,000, you are eligible for First-Time Buyer Relief (since the purchase price is £625,000 or less).

Based on current SDLT rates:
- **0%** on the first **£425,000**: £0
- **5%** on the remaining **£85,000** (the portion from £425,001 to £510,000): £4,250

**Total Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT): £4,250**

*Note: This calculation assumes the property is in England or Northern Ireland. If the property is in Scotland or Wales, different land taxes (LBTT or LTT) apply.*
