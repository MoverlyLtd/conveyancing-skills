If you’re buying for **£510,000** in **England/Northern Ireland**, the **Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) would be £15,500**.

Because the price is **over £500,000**, **first-time buyer relief can’t be used** (FTB relief only applies up to £500,000). So the purchase is charged at the **standard residential rates**.

## Band-by-band breakdown (standard rates)
- **£0–£125,000** at **0%** = **£0**
- **£125,001–£250,000** at **2%** = **£2,500**
- **£250,001–£510,000** at **5%** = **£13,000**

**Total SDLT: £15,500**

**Effective rate:** £15,500 ÷ £510,000 ≈ **3.03%**

## Reminder
SDLT is normally due and the return must be filed **within 14 days of completion**.
