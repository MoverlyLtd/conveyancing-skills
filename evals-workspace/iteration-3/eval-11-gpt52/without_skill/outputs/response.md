Assuming the purchase is in **England or Northern Ireland**, you’re a **genuine first‑time buyer**, and the flat will be your **only/main home** (so no 3% additional-property surcharge), you can use **first‑time buyer SDLT relief**.

**First‑time buyer SDLT bands (England/NI):**
- **0%** on the first **£425,000**
- **5%** on the portion from **£425,001 to £625,000**
- Relief only applies if the price is **£625,000 or less**

**On a £510,000 purchase:**
- 0% on £425,000 = **£0**
- 5% on £510,000 − £425,000 = 5% of £85,000 = **£4,250**

**Stamp Duty (SDLT) due: £4,250**
