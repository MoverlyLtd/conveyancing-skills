The Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) on a purchase price of £510,000 would be **£15,500**.

First-Time Buyer Relief is not applicable in this case, as the purchase price exceeds the £500,000 limit for the relief. The calculation is based on standard residential rates.

### Calculation Breakdown

*   **£0 – £125,000** at 0% = £0
*   **£125,001 – £250,000** (£125,000) at 2% = £2,500
*   **£250,001 – £510,000** (£260,000) at 5% = £13,000

**Total SDLT payable: £15,500**

The effective tax rate for this purchase is 3.03%.

Please note that the SDLT return must be filed with HMRC and the tax paid within 14 days of the completion of the purchase.
